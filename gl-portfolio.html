Reflections

Week 1 : Learning Contract and 1st Consultation

During week 1, my Learning Facilitator and I discussed my learning contract, this learning contract planned out my roadmap and 'to-do list' for the entire guided learning subject. Initially, I really wanted to create a 15 minute long video essay to explain the intricasies of "Preachers Daughter", however my LF voiced her concerns that I may not be able to complete the project due to time and coursework constraints. After the consultation, I reflected and realised that I was having really high expectations for myself to flim the video but her feedback caused me to relax and place less pressure on myself to do a 15 minute long video, instead, I started considered filming a shorter video.

Week 2 + 3 : Website Design on Figma + Linkedin Learning

Week 2 was when I created a website shell on figma, many of the elements were tough to configure such as, figuring out scrolling and linking pages together. To resolve this problem, I watched videos on figma on YouTube to more efficiently design websites. I also researched online for reusable Figma libraries that contain elements I can consistently reuse, this improves efficiency as I don‚Äôt need to copy and paste from flaticon, which I have been doing.

Week 3 : LinkedIn Learning Videos

After watching the videos on LinkedIn Learning I came to the conclusion that the length of the video wasn't very important. I learnt that the most important things are my target audience and end goal. Therefore instead of creating a long video, I decided to do a short hook video that can be incorporated into my website. This video will be in the format of a news report.

Week 4 : Script Creation & Video Filming

I typically use LLMs to generate scripts. However this time I gave myself a challenge, instead of completely relying on chatgpt to ideate and editing afterwards. I decided to ideate on my script before using ChatGPT to improve it.

Week 5 : Website Coding

While coding I would usually use LLMs however, I want to learn how to code without LLMs so instead I used W3 School as resource to customizd the CSS (styles) of the website. Although LLMs are much faster, I think it's important to have a clear idea of the basics before letting LLMs do all the work because I wont learn anything this way.
